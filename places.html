Places to visit in Taiwan

  1. Taipei 101 - The world's second tallest skyscraper.
  2. Sun Moon Lake - A splendid beautiful lake with gondolas.
  3. National Palace Museum - A large art musuem with over 8,000 years of Chinese history.
  4. Shilin Night Market - Taiwan's most famous night market, with a variety of Taiwan's delicacies.
  5. Yushan National Park - Taiwan's largest national park, home to Jade Mountain, the tallest peak of East Asia.
  6. Anping District - A historical district known for its architectural history and styles ranging from Dutch to Koxinga to Qing to Japanese colonial eras.
  7. Kaohsiung - A subtropical metropolis in Southern Taiwan.
